Best Spring Break Destinations Within Driving Distance

  • Nashville, Tennessee

    Nashville is just 4 hours south of Indianapolis, making it the closest warm-weather escape on this list. Spring highs sit in the mid-60s, and the city's live music, food scene, and walkable downtown fill a long weekend or a full week. The easiest spring break road trip from Indianapolis.

  • Gatlinburg, Tennessee

    About 6 hours from Indianapolis, Gatlinburg gives you front-door access to the Great Smoky Mountains. Spring temperatures in the upper 50s to low 60s bring wildflower blooms across the park. Ideal for hikers and families looking for a nature-focused spring break.

  • Asheville, North Carolina

    Asheville is roughly 7 hours southeast of Indianapolis through beautiful mountain terrain. Temperatures in the low 60s, a thriving craft beer scene, and the Blue Ridge Parkway make it a rewarding drive. One of the better warm places to drive for spring break from the Midwest.

  • Hot Springs, Arkansas

    Around 8 hours southwest, Hot Springs delivers spring temperatures in the low 70s plus natural thermal baths along historic Bathhouse Row. The Ouachita National Forest offers trails and scenic drives. An underrated warm road trip within driving distance of Indianapolis.

  • Savannah, Georgia

    Savannah is about 10 hours south of Indianapolis, where spring means mid-70s and azaleas in full bloom. The cobblestone squares, River Street, and Lowcountry cuisine make the longer drive worthwhile. A great option for spring break road trips from Indianapolis when you want real warmth.
