Best Spring Break Destinations Within Driving Distance

  • Nashville, Tennessee

    Nashville is just 5 hours from St Louis, making it one of the easiest warm-weather drives on this list. Spring highs in the mid-60s, plus live music, hot chicken, and walkable neighborhoods, make Nashville a go-to spring break destination. The most convenient spring break road trip from St Louis.

  • Hot Springs, Arkansas

    Hot Springs sits about 5 hours southwest of St Louis and reaches the low 70s during spring. Bathhouse Row, mountain trails in the national park, and a quirky downtown create a relaxing warm-weather escape. An underappreciated warm place to drive for spring break from the Gateway City.

  • Gatlinburg, Tennessee

    Around 7 hours from St Louis, Gatlinburg offers the Great Smoky Mountains at the height of spring wildflower season. Temperatures in the upper 50s to low 60s and uncrowded trails make it a strong pick for nature lovers heading south for warmer weather.

  • Dallas, Texas

    Dallas is roughly 9 hours from St Louis with spring temperatures in the mid-70s. The Deep Ellum district, world-class museums, and Texas BBQ make it a lively spring break destination. Close enough for a comfortable one-day drive that puts you in genuine warmth.

  • Austin, Texas

    About 12 hours south, Austin rewards the drive with spring highs near 80°F. South Congress, live music venues, and Barton Springs Pool deliver one of the warmest road trips within driving distance of St Louis. Ideal for a full week of spring break sunshine.
