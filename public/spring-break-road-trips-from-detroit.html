Best Spring Break Destinations Within Driving Distance

  • Nashville, Tennessee

    Nashville is roughly 8 hours from Detroit and offers spring temperatures in the mid-60s. The honky-tonks on Broadway, local hot chicken spots, and walkable neighborhoods make it an easy spring break pick. A reliable destination for spring break road trips from Detroit when you want warmth without flying.

  • Asheville, North Carolina

    About 9 hours from Detroit through the Appalachian foothills, Asheville warms into the low 60s during spring. The craft beer scene is nationally recognized, and the surrounding Blue Ridge Mountains offer scenic drives and hikes. A strong option among warm places to drive for spring break.

  • Gatlinburg, Tennessee

    Gatlinburg is around 8 hours from Detroit and serves as the main entrance to Great Smoky Mountains National Park. Spring wildflowers and temperatures in the upper 50s to low 60s make it ideal for hikers and nature lovers looking to trade Michigan gray for mountain green.

  • Charleston, South Carolina

    At roughly 11 hours, Charleston requires a longer drive but rewards you with spring temperatures in the low 70s. Cobblestone streets, plantation gardens in bloom, and waterfront dining make it one of the most charming warm road trips within driving distance of Detroit.

  • Savannah, Georgia

    Savannah is about 12 hours south of Detroit, where spring break means mid-70s and blooming azaleas. The tree-lined squares, River Street, and outstanding Southern food make the drive worthwhile. Split the trip over two days and enjoy the warmth all week.
