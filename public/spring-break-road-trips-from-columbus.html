Warm Spring Break Road Trips from Columbus

Columbus sits in the heart of Ohio where March still feels like winter. But its central location puts you within a day's drive of some genuinely warm spring break destinations. Several of the best warm road trips within driving distance of Columbus lead south through the Appalachians to milder climates.

Best Spring Break Destinations Within Driving Distance

  • Gatlinburg, Tennessee

    Just 5 hours south of Columbus, Gatlinburg is the quickest route to spring warmth. Temperatures climb into the upper 50s and low 60s, and the Great Smoky Mountains are alive with wildflowers. An easy weekend trip or the start of a longer spring break road trip from Columbus.

  • Nashville, Tennessee

    Nashville is about 6 hours from Columbus, with spring highs in the mid-60s. Live music, local BBQ, and walkable neighborhoods like 12 South and Germantown make it ideal for a spring break getaway. One of the most popular warm places to drive for spring break from the Midwest.

  • Asheville, North Carolina

    Around 6 hours southeast of Columbus, Asheville offers mountain scenery, a thriving food scene, and spring temperatures in the low 60s. The Blue Ridge Parkway and Biltmore Estate are both within easy reach. A rewarding destination for anyone looking for warm road trips within driving distance.

  • Charleston, South Carolina

    Charleston is roughly 8 hours from Columbus and greets spring visitors with temperatures in the low 70s. The historic district, rainbow row, and fresh Lowcountry seafood deliver a proper warm-weather escape. Worth every mile of the drive south through the mountains.

  • Savannah, Georgia

    About 9 hours south, Savannah's spring weather reaches the mid-70s with blooming azaleas throughout the historic squares. River Street, Forsyth Park, and the local food scene make Savannah a standout among spring break road trips from Columbus for those who want real warmth.
