Best Spring Break Destinations Within Driving Distance

  • Hot Springs, Arkansas

    Hot Springs is just 5 hours south of Kansas City and reaches the low 70s in spring. Bathhouse Row, hiking in the Ouachita Mountains, and the small-town charm make it a quick warm-weather getaway. One of the easiest spring break road trips from Kansas City for a long weekend.

  • Dallas, Texas

    About 8 hours south, Dallas delivers mid-70s spring temperatures and a packed lineup of food, culture, and nightlife. The Fort Worth Stockyards, Deep Ellum, and Perot Museum give you options rain or shine. A dependable warm place to drive for spring break from KC.

  • Nashville, Tennessee

    Nashville is roughly 8 hours east of Kansas City with spring highs in the mid-60s. Live music, local restaurants, and walkable neighborhoods make Nashville an easy recommendation. The I-70 to I-65 corridor makes the drive straightforward.

  • Austin, Texas

    Around 10 hours south of Kansas City, Austin's spring temperatures reach near 80°F. South Congress Avenue, Barton Springs, and the live music scene make Austin one of the best warm road trips within driving distance for spring break. Worth a full week visit.

  • Gatlinburg, Tennessee

    Gatlinburg is about 10 hours from Kansas City, sitting at the entrance to the Great Smoky Mountains. Spring wildflower hikes and temperatures in the upper 50s to low 60s offer a mountain escape that feels a world away from the Kansas plains.
