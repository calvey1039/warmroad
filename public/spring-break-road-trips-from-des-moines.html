Best Spring Break Destinations Within Driving Distance

  • Hot Springs, Arkansas

    Hot Springs is about 8 hours south of Des Moines and reaches the low 70s during spring. Natural thermal baths, Bathhouse Row, and trails through the Ouachita National Forest create a relaxed warm-weather escape. A great spring break road trip from Des Moines for those who like smaller towns.

  • Nashville, Tennessee

    Nashville sits roughly 8 hours southeast of Des Moines with spring temperatures in the mid-60s. The music scene, local food, and walkable districts like the Gulch and Germantown make Nashville a spring break favorite. One of the most popular warm places to drive for spring break from Iowa.

  • Dallas, Texas

    About 11 hours south, Dallas offers spring break temperatures in the mid-70s. Fort Worth Stockyards, Deep Ellum, and the Dallas food scene give you plenty to experience. A comfortable two-day drive that puts you in genuine Texas warmth for the week.

  • Gatlinburg, Tennessee

    Gatlinburg is around 11 hours from Des Moines and opens the door to Great Smoky Mountains National Park. Spring wildflower hikes and temperatures in the upper 50s to low 60s trade Iowa flatlands for mountain scenery. One of the more scenic warm road trips within driving distance.

  • Austin, Texas

    At roughly 13 hours, Austin rewards the longer drive with spring highs near 80°F. South Congress shopping, Barton Springs Pool, and live music venues on Sixth Street make Austin a top-tier spring break destination from Des Moines for anyone willing to make the trip.
