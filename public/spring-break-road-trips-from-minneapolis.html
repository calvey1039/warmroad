Warm Spring Break Road Trips from Minneapolis

Minneapolis spring breaks often come with snow still on the ground. When you are ready to chase warmer weather, driving south opens up real options. WarmRoad helps you find warm places to drive for spring break by tracking live weather along your route and at your destination. The drives are longer from Minneapolis, but the payoff in temperature is significant. These warm road trips within driving distance put you in spring weather that actually feels like spring.

Best Spring Break Destinations Within Driving Distance

  • Hot Springs, Arkansas

    Hot Springs is roughly 10 hours south of Minneapolis and reaches the low 70s during spring. Natural thermal baths on Bathhouse Row, Ouachita National Forest trails, and a laid-back downtown make it a rewarding destination. A strong pick for spring break road trips from Minneapolis when you want warmth without heading to Texas.

  • Nashville, Tennessee

    About 11 hours from Minneapolis, Nashville's mid-60s spring weather feels tropical compared to Minnesota. Broadway honky-tonks, local restaurants, and neighborhoods like Germantown and 12 South keep you busy. One of the most popular warm places to drive for spring break from the upper Midwest.

  • Gatlinburg, Tennessee

    Gatlinburg is around 13 hours from Minneapolis, at the doorstep of Great Smoky Mountains National Park. Spring wildflowers and temperatures in the upper 50s to low 60s offer a dramatic change from the frozen North. Best suited for a full-week spring break trip.

  • Dallas, Texas

    Dallas sits about 14 hours south of Minneapolis, where spring break means mid-70s and sunny skies. The arts district, Fort Worth Stockyards, and Texas BBQ make it an exciting warm-weather destination. Split the drive over two days and enjoy the Texas warmth all week.

  • Austin, Texas

    At roughly 16 hours, Austin is a commitment from Minneapolis, but spring temperatures near 80°F reward the effort. Barton Springs, South Congress shopping, and the live music scene make Austin one of the best warm road trips within driving distance for those willing to log the miles.
