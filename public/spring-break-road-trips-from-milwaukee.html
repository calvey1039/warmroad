Best Spring Break Destinations Within Driving Distance

  • Nashville, Tennessee

    Nashville is about 8 hours south of Milwaukee and delivers spring temperatures in the mid-60s. Live music, local BBQ joints, and neighborhoods like East Nashville and the Gulch give you plenty to explore. A reliable pick for spring break road trips from Milwaukee when you want warmth and culture.

  • Hot Springs, Arkansas

    Around 10 hours southwest, Hot Springs offers natural thermal baths and spring temperatures in the low 70s. Bathhouse Row, the surrounding national forest, and a relaxed pace make it a unique warm-weather destination. An overlooked gem among warm places to drive for spring break.

  • Gatlinburg, Tennessee

    Gatlinburg is roughly 10 hours from Milwaukee, sitting at the entrance to the Great Smoky Mountains. Spring wildflower season brings temperatures in the upper 50s to low 60s, and the park's trails are far less crowded than in summer. A solid nature escape from the Wisconsin cold.

  • Dallas, Texas

    Dallas sits about 14 hours south of Milwaukee, where spring break temperatures reach the mid-70s. The Deep Ellum music district, Fort Worth Stockyards, and Texas-style barbecue make the journey worthwhile. Break the drive into two days for a comfortable warm road trip within driving distance.

  • Austin, Texas

    At roughly 16 hours, Austin is the farthest destination on this list but also the warmest, with spring highs near 80°F. South Congress, Barton Springs Pool, and the live music capital reputation make Austin a premier spring break destination. Best suited for a full-week trip from Milwaukee.
